THEY CALL YOU LEAK
Your name is forgotten in the mud, dirt, and blood. You are simply "Leak".
You joined this hellish war for some reason, but you don't remember it. The endless fighting has left you with a Slaggvara shrapnel protruding from your head like a demon horn, promising certain death if removed.
To deal with the atrocities of wartime, you cope by burning through an entire pack of smokes in a night.
This war has rattled you to the core. Start with 1 (d2) SHOCK and THE SHAKES. Always have last initiative.
GRIZZLED VETERAN
It's rare that a grvnt live to fight another day, but then - with the Dying Lands constantly at war - there's as much opportunity to live as to die. Oftentimes they're one and the same. Such experience is valued among the sea of countless conscripts who reek fo pissed trousers. What doesn't kill you makes a great story.
Your last combat experience: A clandestine mission to sabotage the enemy's SLAGGVARA deposit. Only you survived.
Bear-zerker Rage. Your jaundiced jingoism has clouded your mind, replacing rational thought with only anger. Always attack twice per turn, once ranged and once in melee.
