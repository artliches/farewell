THEY CALL YOU RETCH
Your name is forgotten in the mud, dirt, and blood. You are simply "Retch".
You joined this hellish war after rising from the dead, resuming your past life. The endless fighting has left you with a flayed scalp, dangles in the wind.
To deal with the atrocities of wartime, you cope by writing letters to no one.
SADISTIC SOLDIER
Pleasure is sweetest when paid for by another's pain. Where you were once punished for your psychotically cruel crimes, you now have been loosed upon the world; the only boundaries to your carnage are your own capabilities. Also, your choice in jewelry is questionable at best.
Start with a Punch Dagger. All your melee weapons do +d2 damage.
When killing an enemy with a melee weapon, roll d6 to see what you have collected:
  1. EARS. The screams of the battlefield are horrific, so you’re actually doing them a favor. For every ear you collect: +1 Agility and -1 Toughness
  2. EYES. They really wouldn’t want to see what you’re about to do. For every eye you collect: +1 Presence and -1 Strength
  3. TEETH. This one was way too mouthy. For every tooth you collect: +1 Tier of Armor and -1 max HP
  4. NOSES. The stench of death is no longer of their concern. For every nose you collect: +1 Toughness and -1 Agility
  5. FINGERS. They don’t need to pull a trigger anymore. For every finger you collect: +1 damage and -1 Tier of Armor
  6. TONGUES. You just really like tongues. They’re neat. For every tongue you collect: +1 max HP and -1 Presence
Drop undesired collectables whenever you rest. You can NEVER be Promoted.
But what you REALLY like are Bones. You make sweet musicks with the hollowed out structures of former humans. Prolonged playing drives listeners mad.
