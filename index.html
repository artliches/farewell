THEY CALL YOU LAZY
Your name is forgotten in the mud, dirt, and blood. You are simply "Lazy".
You joined this hellish war for some reason, but you don't remember it. The endless fighting has left you with a hole in your skull that causes helmets to never fit right.
To deal with the atrocities of wartime, you cope by meticulously and eerily cleaning weapons.
This war has rattled you to the core. Start with 1 (d2) SHOCK and THE SHAKES. Always have last initiative.
ARCANE MEDIC
“I solemnly swear to heal others that they may do harm in my stead. I charge boldly towards disease, injury, and death to save those that desire their revenge – and I shall aid the passing of those who do not. This is my Hippocritic Oath.”
Start with one Field Dress Kit and a War Scroll.
From who/where did you learn your medical knowledge?: A highly educated Icefell expat. You euthanized them when they could teach you no more.
The Artist at Work. Your mostly foolproof healing process involves carving small arcane symbols into your patients’ flesh. They gain +d2 healing per day permanently, but roll +1 on the SHOCK table per carving.
