THEY CALL YOU Redolent Commands the Flame
Your name is forgotten in the mud, dirt, and blood. You are simply RedolentCommandsthe Flame.
You joined this hellish war due to your desire for wanton, bloody, terrifying hedonism. The endless fighting has left you with a snaggletooth punctured through your lip.
To deal with the atrocities of wartime, you cope by snorting crushed slagg.
This war has rattled you to the core. Start with 2 (d2) SHOCK and PRIMAL FEAR. Next SHOCK roll is d6.
EMBEDDED ONE
Like all Pale Ones, you are ephemeral, unknowable. Sent by your kin to observe (and participate in) the Dying Lands’ war, you are out of place and time. Your squad views you with varying levels of contempt, fascination, and distrust. What mysteries of these primitive beasts will you solve? For now, you ensconce yourself in their violent ways.
You are infused with ETHER that grants you a GIFT and COVERINGS.
