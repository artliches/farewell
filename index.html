THEY CALL YOU Taciturn Whispers the Beginning
Your name is forgotten in the mud, dirt, and blood. You are simply TaciturnWhispersthe Beginning.
You joined this hellish war because your farmstead was destroyed and your family killed. The endless fighting has left you with a lacerated arm, probably needs treatment.
To deal with the atrocities of wartime, you cope by overindulging in drink until passing out.
This war has rattled you to the core. Start with 1 (d2) SHOCK and THE SHAKES. Always have last initiative.
EMBEDDED ONE
Like all Pale Ones, you are ephemeral, unknowable. Sent by your kin to observe (and participate in) the Dying Lands’ war, you are out of place and time. Your squad views you with varying levels of contempt, fascination, and distrust. What mysteries of these primitive beasts will you solve? For now, you ensconce yourself in their violent ways.
You are infused with ETHER that grants you a GIFT and COVERINGS.
Ontological Oddity. Test Toughness to become invisible (cannot attack or heal).
