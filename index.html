THEY CALL YOU LAZY
Your name is forgotten in the mud, dirt, and blood. You are simply "Lazy".
You joined this hellish war to feed your addiction to the roar of flames and blasts of shrapnel. The endless fighting has left you with a severe case of battle tinnitus that blocks out everything else when in shock.
To deal with the atrocities of wartime, you cope by placing bets with your fellow grvnts.
This war has rattled you to the core. Start with 1 (d2) SHOCK and THE SHAKES. Always have last initiative.
EXPENDABLE SAPPER
Given the highly volatile and sensitive nature of your profession, you find it best to introduce yourself with a bang. You are the life of the party for all the wrong reasons. Where many would opt to go over or around the mountain, you remove it entirely with a fiery blast of raw destructive power - something to remember you by.
Throwing grenades is -2DR and BLAST does not affect you.
Every time you roll Ammo, also roll d3 on Side Arms to restock grenades.
What was the cause of the fondest blast you've ever felt?: That time you threw a Smog Popper into your own barracks during boot camp.
Juggernaut. Clad in solid metal, you bash through walls and anything else in your way. You wear Lobster Armor with a Punch attack (d4, HEAVY). Stick a SLAGG in it: damage becomes d8.
