THEY CALL YOU RAT
Your name is forgotten in the mud, dirt, and blood. You are simply "Rat".
You joined this hellish war because you were exiled from your village and had no where else to go.. The endless fighting has left you with a criss-cross of deep rope burns on your palms.
To deal with the atrocities of wartime, you cope by walking among the corpses, looking for a familiar face.
This war has rattled you to the core. Start with 1 (d2) SHOCK and THE SHAKES. Always have last initiative.
NEW WORLD ENGINEER
Obsessed with progress and the potential applications of SLAGGVARA. You scoff at the old ways and embrace the industrial age of enlightenment, seeking to immortalize yourself through your creations.
You lead two Muskoxen (HP 14) that drag piles of weathered building materials behind them.
You may construct any two of the following once per mission:
  1. Tower of Power.Erect a wooden tower d3 levels high. Firing from the tower adds +1 Presence per level. Falling deals d3 damage per level. Provides Partial cover.
  2. So Hwatcha Want. Construct a mobile rocket battery (2d6 rockets, d2 damage each, IMPACT 2). Costs 1 SLAGG to fire.
  3. No Tanks. Install a row of metal “Dredgehog” tank traps. Armored enemies cannot pass them (lose d2 turns finding a new path).
  4. Bridge the Gap. Quickly assemble a wooden fording bridge across rivers or chasms. Strong enough to hold a squad but not armored units.
  5. Hunker Dory. Pour a stone and metal reinforced box, providing Total Cover and a long, narrow embrasure. Fits two grvnts.
  6. Barbed and Ready. You have a near-unlimited supply of Barbed Wire, and you deploy it like a sculptor (spend 1 Power). All enemies encountering it roll (d3):
    1. d3 damage
    2. Morale
    3. SHOCK
When you embraced your new discipline, you left: A backwards, yokel cult in the forests of Sarkash claiming to possess golden plates etched with forgotten scripture.
