COMBAT AND RESTS

INITIATIVE (d6)

1-3 Enemies act first
4-6 Players act first
Grvnts acting out of turn test Agility+d6

TESTS (d20)

Melee
Test Strength DR12 (± target Cover)
Ranged
Test Presence DR12 (± Aim and target Cover)
Defense
Test Agility DR12 (± your Cover)

CRITS (rolling a natural 20)

Attacking
You may roll damage twice and reduce enemy Armor or Cover by 1 Tier
Defending
You may make an immediate attack

FUMBLES (rolling a natural 1)

Attacking (d4)
  1. You lose all Ammo
  2. You weapon jams or becomes unusable until an action is taken to fix it
  3. You weapon misfires or breaks and can only be repaired after battle
  4. You weapon explodes, dealing its damage to you
Defending
You take double damage and your Armor is reduced by 1 Tier

REACTION (2d6)

*When response is uncertain.
2-3 Attacks on sight
4-6 Prepares for battle
7-8 Shows caution
9-10 Expresses curiosity
11-12 Pleads for help

MORALE (2d6)

Most enemy combatants will not fight to the death if they can save themselves
Roll Morale when:
  • leader is killed
  • half enemy group is killed
  • single enemy has <1/3 HP
If result is higher than enemy's Morale, roll d6:
1-3 Retreats
4-5 Surrenders
6 Switches allegiance

RESTS

Have a smoke, devour a ration: Restore d4 HP
A good night's sleep: Heal d6 HP, relieve d2 SHOCK
Without adequate rations grvnts cannot heal when resting. After two days, starving grvnts lose d4 HP per day
Infected characters do not heal when resting and instead lose d6 HP per day

DOWNED (d6)

*When HP is 0 or -1
1.
Roll a Battle Scar and heal to 3 HP
2.
Roll a Battle Scar and heal to 2 HP
3.
Roll a Battle Scar and heal to 1 HP
4.
Heal to 1 HP and suffer d2 SHOCK
5.
Dishonorable discharge and DEATH
6.
You had a Scream Grenade this whole time?!?!?! You die in the explosion, damaging all nearby

WEAPONS AND ARMOR

IN MELEE

When in melee range:
  • Firearms cannot be reloaded
  • +2 DR to fire

TWO-HANDED

When using a two-handed weapon:
You must spend an action to switch to Firearm/Side Arm/Shield

AIMING

Spend a round to Aim. Gain either:
  • -2 DR to hit
  • +2 damage

ON WATCH

Players may choose to go On Watch. They gain one free ranged attack before rolling Initiative, but cannot Rest

WEAPON KEYWORDS

Listed by order of operation:
SCOPED
Automatic Aim. Cannot be use in melee
BLAST (X)
Deal damage to X targets within blast
IMPACT (X)
Ignore X Tiers of Armor
HEAVY
Degrade cover by one level (hit or miss).
Destroy one Tier of Armor on hit
SHOCK
Force DR14 Toughness test or roll d4 on SHOCK table
BURN (X)
Deal d4 damage for the next X rounds

ARMOR

1. Tier 0. no armor
2. Tier 1. -d2 damage
3. Tier 2. -d4 damage
4. Tier 2. -d2 damage + helmet
5. Tier 3. -d6 damage
6. Tier 3. -d6 damage + helmet
7. Tier 4. -d8 damage
Helmet
Break to prevent all damage from a single attack
Small Shield
-1 damage, break to prevent all damage from melee
Partial cover from ranged
Large Shield
-2 damage, break to prevent all damage from melee
Full cover from ranged

COVER

Partial
±2 DR, wood fence, smoke/GAS, 1/2 covered
Full
±4 DR, trenches, tanks, stone walls, 3/4 covered
Total
Cannot be targeted or damaged, except by weapons with HEAVY
When any weapon with HEAVY attacks a covered target:
Total cover becomes Full
Full cover becomes Partial
Partial cover is destroyed
