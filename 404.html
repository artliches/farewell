THEY CALL YOU JOHNNY
Your name is forgotten in the mud, dirt, and blood. You are simply "Johnny".
You joined this hellish war because you were exiled from your village and had no where else to go.. The endless fighting has left you with a pair of fractured ribs that rattles as you run.
To deal with the atrocities of wartime, you cope by juggling grenades - the more, the better.
This war has rattled you to the core. Start with 1 (d2) SHOCK and THE SHAKES. Always have last initiative.
