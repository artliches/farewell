THEY CALL YOU NOISY
Your name is forgotten in the mud, dirt, and blood. You are simply "Noisy".
You joined this hellish war due to your desire for wanton, bloody, terrifying hedonism. The endless fighting has left you with a nose replaced by a gash.
To deal with the atrocities of wartime, you cope by writing horrible poetry (and forcing otheres to listen to it).
This war has rattled you to the core. Start with 2 (d2) SHOCK and PRIMAL FEAR. Next SHOCK roll is d6.
EXPENDABLE SAPPER
Given the highly volatile and sensitive nature of your profession, you find it best to introduce yourself with a bang. You are the life of the party for all the wrong reasons. Where many would opt to go over or around the mountain, you remove it entirely with a fiery blast of raw destructive power - something to remember you by.
Throwing grenades is -2DR and BLAST does not affect you.
Every time you roll Ammo, also roll d3 on Side Arms to restock grenades.
What was the cause of the fondest blast you've ever felt?: A lucky strike on a Skycrawler with a Boom Lance. Almost earned you a medal.
Juggernaut. Clad in solid metal, you bash through walls and anything else in your way. You wear Lobster Armor with a Punch attack (d4, HEAVY). Stick a SLAGG in it: damage becomes d8.
