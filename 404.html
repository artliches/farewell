THEY CALL YOU LEAK
Your name is forgotten in the mud, dirt, and blood. You are simply "Leak".
You joined this hellish war because as a prisoner you developed an obsession with BRUTALITY AND PAIN. The endless fighting has left you with a SLAGGVARA tattoo, won't stop itching.
To deal with the atrocities of wartime, you cope by carving useless wooden crosses.
This war has rattled you to the core. Start with 2 (d2) SHOCK and PRIMAL FEAR. Next SHOCK roll is d6.
ALTERED MERCENARY
Some consider it a blessing; others find it a cruel revenge of the Dying Lands as destruction ravages the face of the earth. Wildfolk, the SLAGG-cursed, beasts of no nation – these are but a few pejoratives muttered under the breath of those who despise your altered existence. As misunderstood outcasts, Altered Mercenaries wander until someone claims them.
Cannot use Firearms or Tier 2+ Armor
Soldier of what fortune?: Your own life, lest the Inquisitors find you
Hyena-Throated. Your bark is worse than your bite (d6, dealing damage heals d2). The insidious laughter bellowing from your jowls mimics the death knell of dying grvnts. Spend 1 Power and test Presence DR8: cast a random War Scroll.
