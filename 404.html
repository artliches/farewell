THEY CALL YOU KNOT
Your name is forgotten in the mud, dirt, and blood. You are simply "Knot".
You joined this hellish war because an incurable disease ails you - you are not long for this world anyway. The endless fighting has left you with a criss-cross of deep rope burns on your palms.
To deal with the atrocities of wartime, you cope by cataloging every injury in your diary.
HOMESICK RIFLEMAN
Unable to leave home behind—no matter how horrible it was—you took part of it with you to the battlefield. War does not suit you. Oftentimes the gruesome sight of combat induces a deep introspection. Your squad are your siblings now; your written words, your parents.
Start with a Malevolent Rile. Do not roll for Firearms.

The Galgen Beheader A Malevolent Rifle capped with an oversized bayonet (d8), perfect for beheading many an unfortunate grvnt. Melee attacks Crit on an 18-20.
