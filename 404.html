THEY CALL YOU KILLER
Your name is forgotten in the mud, dirt, and blood. You are simply "Killer".
You joined this hellish war due to your desire for wanton, bloody, terrifying hedonism. The endless fighting has left you with a stab wound that never heals, stuffed with gauze.
To deal with the atrocities of wartime, you cope by carving useless wooden crosses.
This war has rattled you to the core. Start with 1 (d2) SHOCK and THE SHAKES. Always have last initiative.
ALTERED MERCENARY
Some consider it a blessing; others find it a cruel revenge of the Dying Lands as destruction ravages the face of the earth. Wildfolk, the SLAGG-cursed, beasts of no nation – these are but a few pejoratives muttered under the breath of those who despise your altered existence. As misunderstood outcasts, Altered Mercenaries wander until someone claims them.
Cannot use Firearms or Tier 2+ Armor
Soldier of what fortune?: A sense of belonging.
Schunk-Striped. Never clean and always reeking of death, you discovered a deterrent is often the best weapon. When you take SHOCK or suffer damage, your body spurts putrid GAS at the damage source.
