THEY CALL YOU Redolent Whispers the Pain
Your name is forgotten in the mud, dirt, and blood. You are simply RedolentWhispersthe Pain.
You joined this hellish war because your lover is fighting on the other side. You want to be close to them - at a bayonet's length. The endless fighting has left you with a broken blood vessel in your left eye.
To deal with the atrocities of wartime, you cope by endlessly counting ammunition.
This war has rattled you to the core. Start with 2 (d2) SHOCK and PRIMAL FEAR. Next SHOCK roll is d6.
EMBEDDED ONE
Like all Pale Ones, you are ephemeral, unknowable. Sent by your kin to observe (and participate in) the Dying Lands’ war, you are out of place and time. Your squad views you with varying levels of contempt, fascination, and distrust. What mysteries of these primitive beasts will you solve? For now, you ensconce yourself in their violent ways.
You are infused with ETHER that grants you a GIFT and COVERINGS.
Ontological Oddity. Test Toughness to become invisible (cannot attack or heal).
