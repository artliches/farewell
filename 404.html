THEY CALL YOU KNOT.
Your name is forgotten in the mud, dirt, and blood. You are simply "Knot".
You joined this hellish war because your farmstead was destroyed and your family killed. The endless fighting has left you with an a set of weak knees and a hunched posture, making you a smaller target.
To deal with the atrocities of wartime, you cope by burning through an entire pack of smokes in a night.
ARCANE MEDIC
“I solemnly swear to heal others that they may do harm in my stead. I charge boldly towards disease, injury, and death to save those that desire their revenge – and I shall aid the passing of those who do not. This is my Hippocritic Oath.”
Start with one Field Dress Kit and a War Scroll.
From who/where did you learn your medical knowledge?: A drunken mortician who always licked their fingers – a habit you picked up.
Leech Therapy. You carry d6 Onda Leeches. Attach to any creature – the creature takes -d2 damage. Will cure most illnesses and lower SHOCK if left on for d2 days; also take -1 to one Stat. You must summon these leeches from the marshes (spend 1 Power: d3 more) or travel to Onda to acquire more.
