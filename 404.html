THEY CALL YOU ROACH
Your name is forgotten in the mud, dirt, and blood. You are simply "Roach".
You joined this hellish war because you are seduced by the morose. The endless fighting has left you with razor wire embedded in your arm (d6).
To deal with the atrocities of wartime, you cope by reading the names on every dog tag you've collected (you must have more).
This war has rattled you to the core. Start with 2 (d2) SHOCK and PRIMAL FEAR. Next SHOCK roll is d6.
CYNICALLY CURSED CHAPLAIN
With terrific violence and unjust cruelty replacing the soft and somber death of the Dying Lands, you have come to a simple conclusion: the gods are dead, and we have killed them. Burning bodies and craven blast marks seed doubt that the Shimmering Fields will not meet the same fate. People do not change. Faith does not stop bullets.
May choose to suffer d2 SHOCK in place of any SHOCK test.
What cursed you with such crippling cynicism?: The horrors of war, utter destruction, and unspeakable violence. The heavens do not seem to care.
Galgorian Chanting. Test Presence DR12 minus current SHOCK while chanting over the body of a recently-deceased ally: all grvnts may reroll Omens, Powers, and Ammo as if it were a new day.
